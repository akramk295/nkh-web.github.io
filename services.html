CLOUDTECH24 OFFERS THE FOLLOWING MICROSOFT LICENSING SERVICES
MICROSOFT BUSINESS BASIC
Microsoft Business Basic (previously Office 365 Business Essentials) is a cloud-based IT solution that offers small and medium-sized businesses a more tech-savvy approach to office work. The solution includes business emails and web-based versions of Microsoft 365 apps such as Excel and Word. It also comes with secure communication and data storage in the form of Microsoft Teams and OneDrive.
The Microsoft Business Basic Package is what you need to boost your company’s productivity and collaboration. It enhances flexibility for remote workers by easing file sharing initiatives through SharePoint, OneDrive, Teams, or through your company’s cloud network.
THE MICROSOFT BUSINESS BASIC PACKAGE HAS THE FOLLOWING FEATURES
SECURE CLOUD STORAGE
You can securely store your business’s data in a single location yet access files from any device, anywhere. This package comes with ITB file storage allowance on OneDrive. Secure cloud storage gives added flexibility in sharing and editing documents while on the go.
BUSINESS EMAIL SYSTEM
Microsoft Outlook offers widespread support across various devices and platforms and the Web using Outlook Web App. This allows for easy and fast access to emails. Outlook allows you to customise your email address to your company name and can hold up to 50GB worth of emails in your inbox and attachments up to 150MB.
WEB APPLICATIONS
This package has web and mobile versions of Word, PowerPoint, Excel and Notebooks. You need a browser to access these programs and work in real-time with other members.
MICROSOFT TEAMS
This complete solution is designed to enable employees to collaborate on projects. Microsoft Teams leverages real-time communication tools like video calling, instant messaging in groups or privately with the ability to share screens.
MICROSOFT BUSINESS STANDARD
Microsoft Business Standard (formerly Office 365 Business Standard) is a widely used Microsoft product catering to small and medium-sized businesses. It adds business emails, data storage in OneDrive and various productivity-enhancing features to the web and desktop versions of the Microsoft Office suite. You also get access to Microsoft Teams. This package allows for collaborative working from different locations. The Microsoft Business Standard package offers the following features:
BUSINESS EMAIL SYSTEM
With Microsoft Office email, you can seamlessly view emails on the web, on mobile devices and the desktop app from anywhere, anytime. This plan offers 50GB of email storage with a 150MB size limit per email.
ALL YOUR FAVORITE MS APPLICATIONS
Enterprises of all scales have a heavy reliance on MS Office. Microsoft Business Standard caters to this demand with offline access to all of your favourite desktop apps. Such apps include Word, PowerPoint and Excel. Additionally, you can also get unrestricted access to Microsoft Access and Publisher.
FLEXIBLE CLOUD STORAGE
The Microsoft Business Standard cloud-based suite of services is designed to help you run a leaner and meaner enterprise. This package comes with 1 TB of file storage on One Drive, making it easy to locate files and data across your organisation.
It also gives remote workers an easy way to access everything from anywhere. Whether it’s in the office or out in the field with mobile devices -all your content can be accessed quickly and efficiently through one platform.
